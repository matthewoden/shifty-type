Paste a game link — three ways in

The link already works when opened; this adds a way to paste one after a reinstall or on a new phone. All three flows share the same sheet (shown once, at the end) — the only real decision is the entry point, and how loudly it announces itself.

Flow A · the quiet pill

Appears only when it's likely needed, then never again

Home is untouched for everyone — the pill renders only in the installed app when there are zero matches stored (a fresh install), and a dismissal hides it for good. The one moment someone needs a paste is the moment it's there.

My take: A and B aren't rivals — sorry, aren't competitors — they compose.
