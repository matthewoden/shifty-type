! Proposed rule change

The challenge resolves itself — no fold, no stand, no waiting

Today a challenge stops the game: the accused player has to come back and pick Fold or Stand before anything resolves — a whole extra async round-trip for a decision that's basically automatic. The referee already knows if the word is real. So skip the ceremony: flag a word and it's settled on the spot. The outcome is a state stamped on the word, not a prompt for the other player.

Flag it→ referee rules → REJECTED (fake) or STANDS (real)
