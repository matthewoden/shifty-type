Shifty Type — the game lobby

Today the home screen resumes one match by its 4-letter code — no idea who you're playing or whose turn it is, and only the most-recent game shows at all. This proposes a lobby that lists every game by the friend's name, splits invites still waiting for a friend from games in play, and floats the ones that need your move to the top. Grey-tagged phone = shipped today, verbatim. Green-ringed = the proposal.
