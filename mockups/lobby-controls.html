Lobby controls — code, edit, delete

Shipped today (grey), the Edit and Have a code? controls are bare text links — no visible "I'm a button" affordance, and Edit is easy to miss. These proposals (green) all give them real, tappable button shapes in the app's own lip-shadow language; they differ in placement and in how delete reads. Delete stays local-only + swipe-first in every version; the question is what the tappable fallback looks like.
